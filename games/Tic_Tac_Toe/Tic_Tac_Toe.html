How to Play

  • Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in empty cells
  • Get three of your marks in a row to win
  • A row can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
  • If all cells are filled with no winner, the game is a draw

Controls

  • Click "Set Player Names" to customize player names
  • Click "New Game" to start a fresh game
  • Click "Reset Scores" to clear game history

Game Rules

  • X always goes first
  • Players cannot place marks in filled cells
  • Game ends when there's a winner or a draw
  • Last 5 games are shown in history

Tips

  • Try to block your opponent's winning moves
  • Control the center cell when possible
  • Watch for diagonal winning opportunities
  • Plan two moves ahead
